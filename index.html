What is in the Syllabus Section?

The Syllabus section provides detailed, branch-wise curriculum information for Diploma and Polytechnic students. Here, you can explore the latest and complete syllabus for various engineering streams including Electrical, Mechanical, Chemical, Computer Science, and Civil Engineering. Each syllabus is organized semester-wise and subject-wise, helping you plan your studies efficiently and stay updated with academic requirements.
